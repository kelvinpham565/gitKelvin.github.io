The Adventures of Scratchity McScratch




Q1: Describe some programming concepts or structures you learned in this unit that you applied in your game.

A1: Programming concepts I learned in this unit that I applied to my game were the stacked if-else structures and the nested if-else structures. I was struggling at first because I relied on stacked if-else structures. Even though the code was technically correct, the sprites did not move as smoothly as expected. However, knowing that I could use nested if-else structures, I utilized them to my ability and made my sprites move smoothly.

Q2: Did you encounter any problems or struggles while working on this project? If you did, how did you solve or overcome them?

A2: Every time I did a code about scoring, the score box does not show up immediately. After saving my project, I refreshed my page a couple of times until the box showed up. Q3: What aspects of the game do you like the best?

A3: I liked how the game's difficulty increases and how the game over and end screens showed up immediately after a particular action was done.
