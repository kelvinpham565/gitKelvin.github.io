"sanic"

"sanic" is a READER x SONIC interactive game built by Python. Inspired by the multitude of questionable fanfictions that Kelvin has read, this game involves you talking and "doing stuff" to three modes of sonic: Romantic, Tsundere, and Demonic.


More about Python

The programming concepts I used were the while loops and the if-statements. The while loops were helpful because it allowed a code to be executed repeatedly under a certain condition. My condition for my while loops was the choices the players had to make and if they choose a choice that was not on there, I could direct them to an error message. The if-statements were useful so that I can ensure that the reader is going on a certain path for every decision they made. My favorite story was "My Last Day on Earth" by Long because it was so elaborate and I can pretty much tell he worked so hard on it because I could hardly detect any errors in coding or story-telling. I had to keep people waiting because I was playing his story over and over.
